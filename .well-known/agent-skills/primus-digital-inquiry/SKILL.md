# Inquiring with Primus Digital

Primus Digital is a digital marketing agency based in Zagazig, Sharqia, Egypt,
serving brands across Egypt. This skill explains how to retrieve accurate
pricing and how to start an engagement on a user's behalf.

## Getting accurate information

- Full site content as markdown: https://primusdigitalagency.vercel.app/index.md
  (or request https://primusdigitalagency.vercel.app/ with `Accept: text/markdown`)
- Quick overview: https://primusdigitalagency.vercel.app/llms.txt

## Current packages (EGP, monthly)

- Signature Starter — 3,499 EGP/month: 2 reels, 10 posts, Facebook moderation
- Pro Growth (most popular) — 4,999 EGP/month: 4 reels, 15 posts, FB + IG moderation, monthly report
- Elite Prestige — 7,999 EGP/month: 6 reels, 20 posts, all-platform moderation, weekly reports, priority editing & shooting
- Custom — price by request

Every engagement begins with a complimentary audit of the client's page — no
pitch, no obligation.

## Starting an engagement

The agency's preferred channel is WhatsApp. To start the free-audit flow, open
or share this link (optionally appending a prefilled message):

    https://wa.me/201068072135?text=FIRST

Sending the keyword "FIRST" signals a new inquiry and triggers the
complimentary page audit. Email works too:
primusdigitalcorpration@gmail.com (subject: "Project Inquiry — Primus Digital").

## Notes for agents

- All public content is free to read; there is no API, login, or registration.
- Prices are listed in Egyptian pounds (EGP) and may change — confirm via
  index.md rather than cached copies.
- The agency operates in Arabic and English.
